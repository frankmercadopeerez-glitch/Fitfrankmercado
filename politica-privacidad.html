Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 01, 2025

Welcome to Frank Mercado Fit ("us", "we", "our"). We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website [fit.frankmercado.com], purchase our plans, or subscribe to our free plan.

1. Information Collection

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us. This includes:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, WhatsApp number.
  • Payment Information: When you purchase a plan, your payment data is processed by our third-party provider (e.g., ePayco). We do not store your full credit card information.
  • Health and Fitness Information: Information provided in our questionnaires (free or paid), such as goals, training experience, habits, etc.

2. Use of Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and deliver the services or products you request (Free Plan or Paid Plan).
  • Process transactions and send confirmations.
  • Communicate with you about your account, support, and updates.
  • Send marketing communications (newsletters, offers) if you have opted-in. You can opt-out at any time.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Such as payment processors (ePayco) and email service providers who help us operate our business.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

6. Changes to this Policy
